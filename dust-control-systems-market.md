<p><p>Dust control systems encompass various market types, including mobile controllers, handheld devices, self-propelled units, tractor-mounted systems, trailed setups, and fixed controllers. Mobile controllers are versatile and can be easily transported, while handheld devices offer targeted application for small areas. Self-propelled units provide autonomous operation for larger spaces. Tractor-mounted systems utilize existing machinery for dust suppression, whereas trailed units are towed by vehicles for flexibility. Fixed controllers are installed permanently, suitable for ongoing dust management in specific locations. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
