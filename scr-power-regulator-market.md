<p><p>SCR power regulators are devices that control power flow in electrical systems using silicon-controlled rectifiers. The market is segmented into two primary types: simple phase and three-phase regulators. Simple phase regulators manage single-phase loads, suitable for residential or small industrial applications, providing precise control over electric power. In contrast, three-phase regulators are designed for larger industrial settings, handling more complex loads with higher efficiency and better performance, ensuring balanced power distribution and improved control over electrical processes.</p></p>
