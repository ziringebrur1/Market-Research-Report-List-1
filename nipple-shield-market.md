<p><p>The nipple shield market comprises two primary types: the regular nipple shield and the contact nipple shield. Regular nipple shields are designed to assist mothers with breastfeeding challenges, offering a barrier that helps infants latch onto the breast. These shields come in various sizes and shapes. In contrast, contact nipple shields are specifically manufactured to facilitate skin-to-skin contact while nursing, promoting closeness between mother and baby. Each type addresses distinct needs in breastfeeding support, enhancing the overall lactation experience.</p></p>
