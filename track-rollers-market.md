<p><p>The track rollers market is primarily divided into two types: stud type track rollers and yoke type track rollers. Stud type track rollers feature a cylindrical design with a fixed stud, providing robust support for linear motion applications. They are often used in heavy machinery and equipment. In contrast, yoke type track rollers have a yoke design that allows for more flexibility and easier mounting, making them suitable for various conveyor systems and light-duty applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
