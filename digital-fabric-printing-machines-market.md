<p><p>Digital fabric printing machines encompass various market types tailored for specific applications. Sublimation inkjet printing involves transferring dye onto fabric via heat, ideal for vibrant, all-over designs on synthetic materials. Direct to fabric printing, suitable for a range of textiles, prints directly onto fabrics using inkjet technology, offering flexibility in design and material. Direct to garment printing focuses on apparel, applying ink directly onto garments, enabling high-quality, detailed designs. Each method serves diverse sectors, enhancing creative possibilities in fashion and textiles.</p></p>
