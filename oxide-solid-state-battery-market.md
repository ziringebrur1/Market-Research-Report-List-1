<p><p>The oxide solid-state battery market primarily consists of two types: all-solid-state batteries and semi-solid batteries. All-solid-state batteries utilize solid electrolytes entirely, enhancing safety and energy density while reducing risks of leakage and flammability. In contrast, semi-solid batteries combine liquid and solid components, offering a balance between traditional lithium-ion batteries and full solid-state technologies. Both types aim to improve performance, longevity, and safety in energy storage applications, appealing to various industries, including electric vehicles and portable electronics.</p></p>
