<p><p>The packaging barrier films market encompasses various types designed to provide protection against moisture, gases, and light. Metallized films use a metallic layer for enhanced barrier properties and aesthetics. Inorganic oxide coated films apply a thin layer of materials like aluminum oxide for superior oxygen resistance. Organic coated/laminated films involve polymer layers to improve durability and performance. Coextruded films combine multiple layers of different polymers to optimize barrier characteristics, flexibility, and mechanical strength, catering to diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
