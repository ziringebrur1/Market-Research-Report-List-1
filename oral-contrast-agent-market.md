<p><strong>The Oral Contrast Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Barium-based Contrast Media</li><li>Iodinated Contrast Media</li><li>Gadolinium-based Contrast Media</li><li>Microbubble Contrast Media</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral contrast agent market encompasses several types tailored for imaging procedures. Barium-based contrast media are primarily used for gastrointestinal studies, enhancing the visualization of digestive tract structures. Iodinated contrast media, suitable for various imaging modalities, are utilized in both oral and intravenous applications to improve vascular and organ imaging. Gadolinium-based contrast media are mainly employed in magnetic resonance imaging, offering high contrast for soft tissue evaluation. Microbubble contrast media are innovative agents enhancing ultrasound imaging by improving the visibility of blood flow and tissue perfusion.</p></p>
