<p><p>The laser diffraction equipment market encompasses various particle analysis methods, each tailored for different applications. Wet particle analysis involves suspending particles in a liquid, ideal for measuring fine particles in slurry forms. Dry particle analysis, on the other hand, focuses on analyzing powder or solid samples, suitable for industries like pharmaceuticals. Spray particle analysis assesses aerosolized particles, critical in sectors such as spray coatings and pharmaceuticals, ensuring quality and consistency in products. Each method caters to specific industry needs, enhancing material characterization.</p></p>
