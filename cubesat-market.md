<p><p>CubeSat is a type of miniaturized satellite commonly used for space research and technology demonstration. Typically measuring just 10 cm x 10 cm x 10 cm per unit, CubeSats can be configured into larger systems by combining multiple units, allowing for a range of applications from Earth observation to scientific research and communication. Their low-cost and relatively simple design make them accessible for universities, private companies, and government agencies.</p><p>The CubeSat market is experiencing significant growth, driven by advancements in satellite technology, increasing demand for small satellite launches, and expanding applications in various sectors, including telecommunications, Earth monitoring, and disaster management.
