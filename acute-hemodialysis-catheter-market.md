<p><p>The Acute Hemodialysis Catheter market comprises two main types: short-term catheters and long-term catheters. Short-term catheters are typically used for immediate dialysis needs, often in emergency situations, and are designed for use over a period of days to weeks. In contrast, long-term catheters are intended for extended use, generally several weeks to months, and are suitable for patients requiring ongoing dialysis treatment. Both types are crucial for facilitating effective renal replacement therapy in patients with acute kidney injury.</p></p>
