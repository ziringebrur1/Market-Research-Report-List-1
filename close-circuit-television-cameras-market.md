<p><p>The Close Circuit Television (CCTV) camera market features various types catering to specific surveillance needs. Bullet cameras, characterized by their cylindrical shape, are designed for outdoor use, providing long-range visibility. PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom) cameras offer flexibility with adjustable viewing angles and zoom capabilities, ideal for monitoring large areas. Other types can include thermal cameras or covert cameras, targeting specialized applications in security and surveillance.</p></p>
