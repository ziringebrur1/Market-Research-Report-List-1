<p><strong>The Building Fire Retardant Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building fire retardant coatings market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based products. Water-based coatings offer lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them more environmentally friendly and safer for indoor applications. They dry quickly and provide adequate fire resistance. In contrast, solvent-based coatings typically deliver superior performance in terms of durability and resistance to moisture and wear, making them suitable for exterior applications. Both types are crucial for enhancing fire safety in construction materials.</p></p>
