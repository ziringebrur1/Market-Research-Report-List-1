SCADA systems manage large-scale processes across distributed geographical locations, while DCS focuses on single-site industrial processes, ensuring optimized operations within facilities. PLCs are used for automation, controlling machinery in real-time. Each type requires tailored security solutions to protect against cyber threats and ensure operational integrity in critical infrastructure.</p></p>
