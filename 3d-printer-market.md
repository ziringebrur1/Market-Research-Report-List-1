<p><strong>The 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDM（Fused Deposition Model）</li><li>SLA（Stereolithography）</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printer market is primarily categorized into types based on printing technology. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) utilizes thermoplastic materials, extruding melted filament layer by layer, making it popular for prototyping and home use. Stereolithography (SLA) employs a laser to cure liquid resin into solid form, producing high-resolution parts with intricate details, often used in dental and jewelry applications. Each type serves distinct needs, with FDM being more accessible and cost-effective, while SLA offers superior accuracy and finish.</p></p>
