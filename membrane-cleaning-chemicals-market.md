<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Membrane Cleaning Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Caustic</li><li>Oxidants or Disinfectants</li><li>Acids</li><li>Surfactants</li><li>Chelating Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The membrane cleaning chemicals market consists of various types designed for efficient membrane maintenance. **Caustic agents** are used to remove organic fouling and scale. **Oxidants or disinfectants** eliminate microorganisms, thereby preventing biofouling. **Acids** effectively dissolve inorganic deposits such as calcium and magnesium scales. **Surfactants** enhance the removal of oily and greasy contaminants by reducing surface tension. **Chelating agents** bind metal ions, preventing scale formation and facilitating cleaner efficacy. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing membrane performance.</p></p>
