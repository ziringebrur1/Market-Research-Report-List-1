<p><p>The 3D Log Scanner market encompasses various technologies, including laser and X-ray scanning. Laser scanners use light beams to capture precise dimensions and surface details of logs, ensuring accurate measurements and efficient processing. In contrast, X-ray scanners utilize radiographic techniques to analyze the internal characteristics of logs, identifying defects and density variations without damaging the material. Both technologies enhance the efficiency and accuracy of log processing in the forestry and wood industries, providing valuable data for resource management and quality control.</p></p>
