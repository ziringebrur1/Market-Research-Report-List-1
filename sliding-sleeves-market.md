<p><p>Sliding sleeves in the oil and gas industry can be categorized into three market types: open/close systems, which allow controlled fluid flow by sliding the sleeve to either open or close the wellbore; choking markets, where the sleeve restricts flow to manage pressure and enhance production efficiency; and squeeze markets, where the sleeve facilitates cementing or sealing operations.
