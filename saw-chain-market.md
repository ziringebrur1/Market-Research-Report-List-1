<p><ul><li>Full Chisel</li><li>Semi Chisel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The saw chain market is primarily divided into two types: full chisel and semi chisel chains. Full chisel chains feature square-cornered cutters, offering fast and aggressive cutting, making them suitable for professionals and hardwood applications. In contrast, semi chisel chains have rounded cutters that provide better durability and performance in dirty or abrasive conditions, making them ideal for general use and softer woods. Each type caters to different user needs, influencing their market demand and application preferences.</p></p>
