<p><p>The malt ingredients market is categorized into three primary types: dry extracts, liquid extracts, and malt flour. Dry extracts consist of concentrated malt sugars, ideal for brewing and baking, offering convenience and storage benefits. Liquid extracts are viscous solutions rich in malt sugars, commonly used in brewing and cooking for their easy incorporation. Malt flour, a finely milled version of malted grains, is utilized in baking and brewing, enhancing flavor and nutritional value in various applications.</p></p>
