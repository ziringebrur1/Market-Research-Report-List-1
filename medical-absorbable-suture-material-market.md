<p><ul><li>Synthetic Material</li><li>Natural Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical absorbable suture material market comprises two main types: synthetic and natural materials. Synthetic absorbable sutures are made from materials like polyglycolic acid and polyglactin, offering consistent absorption rates and minimal tissue reaction. Natural absorbable sutures, derived from biological sources such as collagen, provide excellent biocompatibility but may have variable absorption times. Both types are designed to support wound healing while gradually dissolving in the body, reducing the need for suture removal and minimizing the risk of infection.</p></p>
