<p><p>The Premium ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition) equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: Plasma Enhanced ALD (PEALD) and Thermal ALD. PEALD utilizes plasma to enhance chemical reactions at lower temperatures, improving film quality and uniformity, which is crucial for complex 3D structures in advanced applications. In contrast, Thermal ALD relies on thermal energy to enable precise deposition of materials, making it suitable for a wide range of semiconductor and electronic device manufacturing, where high purity and reliability are essential.</p></p>
