<p><ul><li>PE-RT Oxygen Barrier Pipes</li><li>PEX Oxygen Barrier Pipes</li><li>PB Oxygen Barrier Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiant heating pipe market features three primary types of oxygen barrier pipes: PE-RT, PEX, and PB. PE-RT (Polyethylene of Raised Temperature Resistance) pipes offer flexibility and temperature resilience for radiant heating. PEX (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) pipes are widely used due to their excellent thermal properties and resistance to scale buildup. PB (Polybutylene) pipes are known for their durability and ease of installation. Each type serves different needs in radiant heating systems, ensuring optimal efficiency and performance.</p></p>
