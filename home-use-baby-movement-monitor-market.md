<p><p>The home-use baby movement monitor market features three primary types: Under-The-Mattress Format, Diaper Attachment Format, and Smart Wearable Format. Under-The-Mattress monitors detect movements using sensors placed beneath the crib mattress, ensuring a non-intrusive setup. Diaper Attachment monitors are designed to be attached to the baby's diaper, providing real-time movement tracking. Smart Wearable monitors, such as bands or clips, are worn directly on the baby, offering comprehensive movement data and often integrating with smartphone applications for enhanced monitoring.</p></p>
