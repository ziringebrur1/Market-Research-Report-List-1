<p><p>The thin film pressure sensor market is categorized into two primary signal output types: simulation output signals and digital output signals. Simulation output signals provide an analog representation of pressure changes, allowing for continuous monitoring and real-time data processing. In contrast, digital output signals convert pressure measurements into discrete data, enhancing accuracy and compatibility with digital systems. Both types are essential for various applications, including industrial monitoring, automotive systems, and healthcare, driving demand for advanced sensor technologies.</p></p>
