<p><p>The portable breathing machine market encompasses various types tailored to different respiratory needs. Asthma nebulizers convert liquid medication into mist for easy inhalation, aiding asthma management. CPAP breathing machines deliver continuous positive airway pressure, primarily used for sleep apnea treatment, ensuring airway stability during sleep. BIPAP machines offer bilevel positive airway pressure, providing variable airflow for patients with more complex breathing issues. Other devices may include portable oxygen concentrators and combination therapies, catering to diverse respiratory conditions and patient requirements.</p></p>
