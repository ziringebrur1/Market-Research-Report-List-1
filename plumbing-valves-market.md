<p><strong>The Plumbing Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Globe Valves</li><li>Stop Valves</li><li>Check Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plumbing valves market includes various types, each serving specific functions. Gate valves control flow through lifting a gate, ideal for on/off applications. Globe valves regulate flow with a spherical body shape, allowing precise control. Stop valves, often referred to as shut-off valves, prevent fluid movement entirely. Check valves ensure fluid flows in one direction to prevent backflow. Ball valves utilize a spherical disc for quick on/off control, while butterfly valves feature a rotating disc for regulating flow, suitable for larger pipelines.</p></p>
