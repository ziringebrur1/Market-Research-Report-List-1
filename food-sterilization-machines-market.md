<p><p>The food sterilization machines market includes various types that ensure food safety and prolong shelf life. Heat sterilization uses high temperatures to destroy microorganisms, while steam sterilization employs moist heat for effective sterilization. Radiation sterilization utilizes ionizing radiation to eliminate pathogens, and chemical sterilization involves using chemical agents for microbial control. Filtration sterilization employs membrane filters to remove contaminants from liquids. Other methods may include alternatives like cold plasma or ultraviolet light. Each method offers distinct advantages depending on the food product and preservation needs.</p></p>
