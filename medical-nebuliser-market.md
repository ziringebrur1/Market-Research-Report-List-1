<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebuliser</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebuliser</li><li>Mesh Nebuliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical nebuliser market comprises three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulisers. Pneumatic nebulisers, or jet nebulisers, use compressed air to convert liquid medication into mist. Ultrasonic nebulisers employ high-frequency sound waves to achieve aerosolization, producing a fine mist with less noise. Mesh nebulisers utilize a vibrating mesh to facilitate drug delivery, offering portable options with efficient medication dispersion. Each type caters to different patient needs, preferences, and conditions, driving their diverse adoption in respiratory therapy.</p></p>
