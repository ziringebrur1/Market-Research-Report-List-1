<p><strong>The Cerebral Thrombectomy Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasound-assisted Thrombectomy Systems</li><li>Rheolytic Thrombectomy Systems</li><li>Rotational Thrombectomy Systems</li><li>Aspiration Thrombectomy Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cerebral thrombectomy systems are medical devices used to remove blood clots from the brain. Ultrasound-assisted thrombectomy systems utilize ultrasound waves to help break down clots, improving removal efficiency. Rheolytic thrombectomy systems use high-pressure saline to fragment clots. Rotational thrombectomy systems employ a mechanical rotating device to dislodge and extract clots. Aspiration thrombectomy systems involve suction to vacuum clots out of blood vessels. Other systems may include innovative or hybrid approaches for enhanced thrombectomy procedures, tailoring treatments to patient needs.</p></p>
