<p><p>The biologic excipients market encompasses various types that enhance drug delivery and stability. Polymers serve as stabilizers and control release rates. Sugar alcohols provide protection and maintain moisture. Polysorbates act as surfactants, improving solubility. Inorganic salts help maintain osmotic balance and enhance formulation stability. Amino acids contribute to protein structure and solubility. Surfactants reduce surface tension, aiding in dispersion. Other excipients may include flavoring agents or preservatives, supporting diverse pharmaceutical formulations in biologics.</p></p>
