<p><ul><li>C-band</li><li>X-Band</li><li>Ku-band</li><li>Ka-band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military Satcom terminal systems are categorized based on frequency bands: C-band, X-band, Ku-band, and Ka-band. C-band offers broad coverage and is reliable for long-range communications, while X-band provides better resistance to jamming and is suited for tactical operations. Ku-band delivers high data rates, ideal for video and imagery transmission, and is widely used for broadband connectivity. Ka-band supports high-capacity communication with advanced technologies, making it suitable for high-throughput and real-time applications in modern military operations.</p></p>
