<p><p>The medical electric drill market includes three primary types: pneumatic drills, electric drills, and battery-powered drills. Pneumatic drills utilize compressed air for operation, offering high speed and precision, making them ideal for specific surgical applications. Electric drills are powered by electricity, providing consistent torque and control, suitable for a wide range of surgical procedures. Battery-powered drills offer portability and convenience, allowing for greater maneuverability in various settings, particularly in emergency or field situations where access to power is limited.</p></p>
